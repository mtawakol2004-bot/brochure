Drone Delivery
"Drones will be as common as delivery trucks — but they fly."
Last-mile delivery — getting a package from warehouse to door — is the most expensive part of shipping: up to 53% of total cost. Traditional trucks face urban traffic, rising fuel costs, and driver shortages. Shipping drones are autonomous UAVs that carry packages through the air — no traffic, no delays, no driver needed.
